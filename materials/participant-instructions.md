# Participant Instructions: Ball Task

Printable handout for participants. These instructions match the on-screen
text displayed by PsychoPy during the neurofeedback session
(`psychopy/balltask/rt-network_feedback.py`, lines 524-546).

---

## Before the First Run (No Feedback)

Continue the Mental Noting practice using your chosen anchor. You may
switch anchors at any time.

Two circles and a white ball will appear on the screen. They remain
still during this run.

## Before Every Run

A plus sign (+) appears for 30 seconds at the start. While it is
on screen, relax and wait.

Once the circles appear, begin Noting. Each practice period lasts
2.5 minutes.

## Before the First Feedback Run

The circles and white ball will appear again. This time, the ball
moves according to your brain activity.

The ball moving up toward the top yellow circle means you are in a
mindful brain state. If the ball reaches either circle, it resets to the
center.

Focus on Noting: notice your sensations moment to moment and silently
label each one. Glance at the screen now and then to see where the ball
is heading.

## Before Later Feedback Runs

Practice Noting for another 2.5 minutes. The ball moves upward when
your brain enters the mindful state.

## Before Later No-Feedback Runs

Practice Noting for another 2.5 minutes. The ball and circles remain
still; ignore them.

---

## Post-Run Questions

After each run lasting at least 60 seconds, you answer four slider
questions. Use the left and right buttons to move the slider, then press
the top button to confirm.

1. **How often were you using the mental noting practice?**
   (Never ... Always)

2. **How often did you check the position of the ball?**
   (Never ... All the time)

3. **How difficult was it to apply mental noting?**
   (Not at all ... Very Difficult)

4. **How calm do you feel right now?**
   (Not at all ... Very calm)
